Novel way of learning a language

AA Reader app includes many innovative way of learning, helping you to better learn a language via reading!

1

Reading and learning integrated

Clean, elegant, fully featured and free ebook reader.

2

Tap a word to display the explain inline

Context based explain will display directly at the right side. When it appears again, explain will auto display.

3

Word + sentence list

New word is automatically added to your word list together with the sentence containing it.

4

Chapter summary

A summary page will show after end of each chapter, helping you to revise the new words.

5

Self examination

Give yourself a test while reading, reinforce words learned.

6

Whole sentence/paragraph translation and text-to-speech

FAQ

See if below answers can help

Unable to convert MOBI file?

File converter is provided by third-party, if it takes too long to convert, please try use online tools such as : https://www.online-convert.com

Unable to translate words in PDF document?

We currently do not support scanned PDF document or files generated using special software.

How to customize background and text color?

Tap on your profile image at the bottom right -> App Settings -> Custom Reader Theme

How to prevent unwanted taps when reading?

Tap on your profile image at the bottom right -> App Settings -> Single tap text -> Ignore

How to download Android version?

We do not have an Android version yet, but development is in progress.

How to upload dictionary?

We do not support uploading of your own dictionary file, but you can install offline dictionaries by tapping on profile image at bottom right -> Offline Dictionaries

How to read audio ebooks?

Tap at the bottom of your reader page, tap on Headphone button, select a language.

How to export words to Anki decks?

You can export words to CSV files at Words page -> tap three dots button at top right -> Export to CSV, then import the file in Anki software.

Is the app free?

Yes, it's completed free for now. You can always donate from inside the app.

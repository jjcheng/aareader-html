Introduction

AA Reader (the App) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. The authors of the App is here below known as "we". This privacy policy sets out the basis on data and information the App collects from you.

What kind of data is being collected?

The App collects insensitive “Personal Data” about you when you install and use the App. The nature of the Personal Data that we collect will be determined by how you are using the App. We will only use your Personal Data as set out below and always in accordance with applicable data protection laws. While we are not based in the European Economic Area ("EEA") we also comply with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation 2016/679 (the “EU GDPR”) together with the version of the EU GDPR which is incorporated into the domestic law of the United Kingdom (the “UK GDPR”) (the EU GDPR and the UK GDPR are collectively referred to as the "GDPR"). None of the data collected can be linked to your real life identity.

  • Login/Sign up: we collect your display name (may not be your real name), WeChat hidden ID (not your public WeChat ID) and avatar image url if you are logging in using WeChat, Apple hidden ID (not your public Apple ID) if you are logging in using Apple ID. They are stored in our secured server in Alibaba Cloud.
  • Any request made from the App to our server will include data such as your device model, App version, interface language etc...
  • Ebook conversion: your MOBI/DOCX files are automatically uploaded to our server for conversion, they are deleted immediately after conversion is completed.
  • Translation/Dictionary/Text-to-Speech requests: they are uploaded to our server for processing.
  • In-App-Purchase: buying credits or giving up tips record such as your user ID, amount, datetime are uploaded to our server.
  • Crash data: We use Sentry.io to collect any crash data from the App.
  • If you are using external services in the App such as DeepL translation service, external service provider may collect some of your data such as translation text.

Information that you provide to us:

We collect Personal Data that you voluntarily provide to us when you use the App. For example, you may provide us with your contact information such as your email address when you send us a feedback inside the App, through an online form, or other methods (such as email us directly or contact us through WeChat official account).

What is this data being used for?

We use this data collected to identify you as a user, perform user related tasks and improve your user experience. To be specific:

  • Your login info (hidden Id and avatar image url) is collected to distinguish you from other users.
  • Translation/Dictionary/Text-to-Speech tasks can only be completed via our server.
  • In-App-Purchase data is stored in case of a need to restore purchases.
  • MOBI/DOCX files have to be converted by our server in order to be used in the App.
  • Crash data are used to identify bugs and performance issues so you can enjoy better user experience.

Who is this data disclosed to?

  • We may disclose your Personal Data to third party providers (such as Sentry.io), as well as to law enforcement and regulatory agencies as may be required by law.
  • If you share any of your content in the App using sharing function, those data will be disclosed to the respective recipients.

How long is this data being retained?

Different kind of data will be retained for different amount of time, to be specific:

  • Login info, In-App-Purchase data will be retained until you close the account.
  • Translation/Dictionary/Text-to-Speech requests are not stored.
  • MOBI/DOCX files will be deleted immediately when the conversion is completed.
  • Crash data and other data collected by third-party providers are decided by the respective providers, ususally not more than 3 months.

Is the data secure?

All of the data collected are stored in our secure servers in Alibaba Cloud with all security features being taken into considerations.

How do I remove my data?

At any point of time, you can close your account as shown below. Once account is closed, all your personal data are erased permenantly and cannot be recovered.

  • Tap on your profile photo at bottom right
  • Tap Edit profile
  • Tap Close Account

What data is not collected?

  • We do not collect or ask for any of your sensitive personal data such as email address, real name, phone number, address etc...
  • A lot of other data generated when using the app such as read progress, bookmarks, notes, words etc... are stored on your device or respective cloud storage account, e.g. iCloud for iOS version. We do not collect them in any way.
  • We do not sell your Personal Data collected to any company or individual by any mean.
  • There is absoultely no tracking of your device ID/advertising ID in the App.

Do not agree to the terms above?

If you do not agree to any of the terms above, please close the account and uninstall the App immediately.

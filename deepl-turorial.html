How to use DeepL API in AA Reader

DeepL has been one of my favourite translation tools. The quality of its translation is even better than many of the big names out there. In this tutorial, I will show you how to integrate free DeepL API into AA Reader.

1. Head to DeepL API page, click Sign up for free. DeepL offers free 500k characters translation per month, you can sign up their Pro subscription if you need more.

2. Sign up a DeepL account, I do agree it outperforms many competitors.

3. Key in your particulars and your credit card details for verification purpose, you won't be charged.

4. When sign up is complete, you will see this page.

5. Click Account Management, or go to top right hand corner -> Click your email button -> Account, then Click Account tab

6. Scroll to the bottom you will see your Authentication Key for DeepL API, copy that

7. Open AA Reader app, tap on your profile photo at the bottom right -> Tap External Services

8. Tap DeepL

9. Paste your Authentication Key for DeepL API or manually key in, make sure it's keyed in correctly.

If you are using the free API, make sure Free Account switch is on.

Also, make sure In Use switch is on. If you don't want to use use anymore in future, just turn it off.

Tap Save at top right.

You are done! Now all translation works are done using your DeepL API.

Again, your API keys are kept in your device or your iCloud account, it will never be uploaded to our server. Rest assure and enjoy reading!
